Most Famous Signers

Thomas Jefferson
He was the principal author of the Declaration and is perhaps the most famous signer.
John Adams
A key figure in the American Revolution and a strong advocate for independence, Adams went on to become the second president of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
One of the most well-known Founding Fathers, Franklin was a diplomat, inventor, and a key figure in securing French support during the Revolution.
John Hancock
Known for his large, bold signature, Hancock was the president of the Continental Congress at the time of signing.
Samuel Adams
A leading figure in the push for independence, Adams was also a cousin of John Adams.
Richard Henry Lee
He introduced the motion for independence in the Continental Congress.
