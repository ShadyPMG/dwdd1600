About the Artist

I create emotionally charged, unconventional work that exists between darkness and warmth. My practice explores distortion, rebellion, and vulnerability — embracing imperfection as truth.

Though my visuals may appear intense or strange, the intention behind them is rooted in connection, humanity, and emotional honesty.
